💡 The Problem

College students in Mumbai and Pune struggled to find safe, verified hostel accommodations. The existing system relied on unsafe Facebook groups, word-of-mouth referrals, and unverified listings - leading to last-minute scrambles, safety concerns, and information asymmetry.

Key Insight: Students needed a trusted platform where they could discover, verify, and book hostels near their colleges - all in one place.

🎯 The Solution

As Co-Founder, I led product strategy and built HostelHub from the ground up:

  • Smart Search Algorithm: Proximity-based matching with filters for budget, amenities, and college distance
  • Verification System: Every hostel operator was personally verified to build trust
  • Partnership Strategy: Secured 25+ hostel partnerships with zero marketing budget through strategic timing and incentive alignment
  • User-Centric Design: Simple, mobile-first interface designed for students on the go

🚀 Impact & Growth

Within the first year, HostelHub became a trusted platform for students across Mumbai and Pune:

  • Facilitated 100+ direct bookings through the platform
  • Indirectly helped 500+ students find safe accommodations
  • Built partnerships with 25+ verified hostel operators
  • Zero marketing budget - grew entirely through word-of-mouth and strategic partnerships
  • Maintained high user satisfaction with verified listings and responsive support
The Growth Hack: Leveraged college admission timelines and offered hostel owners guaranteed occupancy during off-season in exchange for exclusive partnerships - creating a win-win that cost ₹0 in acquisition.

🎓 Key Learnings

Building HostelHub taught me invaluable lessons about building marketplaces:

  • Marketplace Density Matters: Two-sided marketplaces need hyperlocal density to achieve sustainable unit economics
  • Trust is Everything: In markets with information asymmetry, verification and reviews are non-negotiable
  • Resourcefulness > Capital: Strategic partnerships and timing can substitute for marketing budgets
  • Founder Alignment: Co-founder alignment on growth strategy is critical - learned when to wind down vs. force misaligned vision
